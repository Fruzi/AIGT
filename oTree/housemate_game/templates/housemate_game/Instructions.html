Instructions

This is a sort of chicken game with 2 players and an uncapped number of possible choices.

Both player prefer the opponent to take out the trash but lose out on rewards the longer the game drags on, when will you give in and take it out yourself?

The time in which the trash is taken out is the lower of the two chosen by the players, and the payoffs determined based on it. Whoever chose the smaller number will have to pay the tax, while the one who chose the bigger one is exempt from it. If both players choose to take out the trash then both pay half the tax
